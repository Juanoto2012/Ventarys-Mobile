Ventarys AI is a cutting-edge mobile chat application for Android, meticulously designed to provide a premium and fluid artificial intelligence experience. Inspired by the minimalist aesthetics of ChatGPT, Ventarys AI combines a clean, modern interface with the power of multiple high-performance language models.

Key Features:

- Modern & Responsive UI: Experience a sleek "GPT-style" interface that looks great on any device, from small phones to large tablets. Full support for Material You and adaptive Light/Dark modes.
- Multiple AI Providers: Choose your favorite AI engine!
    - Ventarys (Free): Our optimized default models, including Codestral and Llama 3, ready to use out of the box.
    - Groq Cloud: Experience lightning-fast inference with Llama 3.1 and Mixtral models.
    - OpenRouter: Access a vast library of free-to-use models from various creators.
    - Hugging Face: Connect with the latest community-driven models.
- Dynamic Model Selection: The app automatically fetches the latest available models from your chosen providers, ensuring you always have access to the newest technology.
- Bring Your Own Key: For power users, securely manage your own API keys for Groq, OpenRouter, and Hugging Face.
- Local Chat History: Keep track of all your conversations. Your chats are saved locally on your device for privacy and convenience.
- Rich Markdown Support: Read beautifully formatted responses with support for bold text, lists, and clickable links.
- Interactive Feedback: Enjoy a fluid experience with an animated three-dot "thinking" indicator and a modern, feature-rich input field.
